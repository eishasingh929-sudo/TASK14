Answer notes
- University admissions usually evaluate academic marks, entrance exams, and document verification.
- A complete application packet includes identity proof, transcripts, and category certificates when applicable.
- Applicants should track deadlines for registration, payment, and counseling rounds.

Important process checkpoints
1. Registration and profile creation
2. Document upload and validation
3. Merit list publication
4. Seat allotment and fee confirmation

Support guidance
- If a student misses a counseling round, they should check the next open round and policy for upgradation.
- If documents are rejected, re-upload within the correction window.
