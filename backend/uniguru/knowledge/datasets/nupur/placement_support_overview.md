Answer notes
- Placement readiness combines resume quality, communication practice, and role-specific interview preparation.
- Students should maintain a project portfolio with measurable outcomes.
- Weekly mock interviews improve confidence and answer clarity.

Placement workflow
1. Resume audit
2. Aptitude practice
3. Technical interview prep
4. HR and behavioral interview prep
5. Offer comparison and acceptance planning

Support guidance
- Focus on one target role at a time for better preparation depth.
- Keep a tracker of applications, interview dates, and recruiter feedback.
